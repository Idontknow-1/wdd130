Arizona, U.S.A

My state is known for being the hottest state in the U.S.A. Temperatures usually rise above the 100 degrees throught the whole summer, which seems to get longer with every passing year. Although very hot, there are many beautiful places to enjoy the heat. You can go tubing down the Salt River or you can go to various lakes around Arizona. The capitol city is Phoenix, which is were i was born. There is always a lot to see. Many enjoy cruising in Downtown Phoenix. Although Arizona is a dessert, many places more up North have beautiful greenery like Sedona and Flaggstaff. For cooler weather and snow, those are the places to be.
